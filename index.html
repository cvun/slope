Description

If you like fast-paced runners that require a lot of focus from the player, then you should pay attention to the game Slope. Here you will obviously spend a lot of time and want to tell all your friends about this game.

Your goal is to pass through obstacles at a very high speed and thus cover a long distance. You need to go as far as possible from the start, spend all your strength on it and get to the furthest distance at any cost (it is worth noting that you can’t get to the end here, the game has an endless track, but this only gives you a reason to move further and further).

Once you learn how to play and become a pro, you should compete for a place on the leaderboard (who knows, maybe you will be able to take first place on this list and then proudly tell your friends about it). If you don't manage to be the best player of all time, then there are also thirty-day lists, seven days. You will definitely be able to hit one of them, and then you will need to gradually develop your skill.

Slope is a great competitive game that can be an enjoyable time killer. This game is not difficult and you do not need to spend a lot of time learning how to play it. Also, you don't need to download or buy anything. Just click on Play in your browser and the game will begin.
